—Our Commitment To Our Investors—



We understand that our investors have worked very hard to generate the capital they entrust to us.

Our investment committee vets all important investment decisions (both when we decide to make or to sell investments) and is regularly updated on how our investment are performing and on all other important matters regarding Referencia International.

To ensure that this vetting is independent, the majority of the votes on our Investment Committee is held by outside members and not by our partners.

We are committed to regular and transparent reporting to our investors, and, as we are a small team, we are always happy to discuss or sit down with you directly to answer any further question you may have.

Most importantly, we are fully committed to the success of our investments and in generating capital return for our investors and our interests are aligned to those of our investors.
